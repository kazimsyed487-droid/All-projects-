Ten legends.

Hand-picked for what each one pushed past — power, speed, or the line between hybrid and hypercar.

01 / MARANELLO, ITALY

Ferrari LaFerrari

963 hpPower
<3.0s0-100 km/h
V12 + KERSPowertrain

The LaFerrari is Maranello's pinnacle hypercar, mating a 6.3-liter V12 engine with a Formula 1-derived HY-KERS electric system. Generating a combined 963 hp, it accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in under 3 seconds and features active aerodynamics alongside a carbon-fiber chassis built in-house.

02 / ÄNGELHOLM, SWEDEN

Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut

1,600 hpPower
330+ mphTop Speed
$3.5MPrice

The Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut is designed to be the fastest production car ever made, targeting a theoretical top speed exceeding 330 mph. Priced around $3.5 million, it features a 5.0L twin-turbo V8 engine producing up to 1,600 horsepower, minimal aerodynamic drag, and a wingless body for ultimate straight-line stability.

03 / MUNICH, GERMANY

BMW M5 (G90)

717 hpPower
3.4s0-60 mph
738 lb-ftTorque

The all-new BMW M5 (G90) is an electrified monster, packing an M HYBRID V8 powertrain that delivers a shattering 717 hp and 738 lb-ft of torque. It launches from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.4 seconds and boasts supercar-level dynamics combined with executive luxury.

04 / WOKING, UNITED KINGDOM

McLaren P1

903 hpPower
2.8s0-100 km/h
375 unitsProduction

The McLaren P1 cemented its legendary status by pairing a 3.8L twin-turbo V8 with an electric motor to deliver 903 hp and 900 Nm of torque. Built between 2013 and 2015, only 375 units were produced, allowing it to hit 0 to 100 kph in just 2.8 seconds.

05 / DEARBORN, UNITED STATES

Ford Mustang Dark Horse

500 hpPower
418 lb-ftTorque
5.0L V8Engine

The Ford Mustang Dark Horse is the pinnacle of naturally aspirated American muscle, pairing a 500-horsepower Gen-4 5.0L Coyote V8 with track-focused aerodynamics and a dual-throttle body intake. It delivers 418 lb-ft of torque and comes equipped with a 6-speed TREMEC manual or 10-speed automatic transmission.

06 / GAYDON, UNITED KINGDOM

Aston Martin Valkyrie

1,160 hpPower
2 tonsDownforce
Hybrid V12Engine

Co-developed with Red Bull Racing Advanced Technology and designed by legendary F1 engineer Adrian Newey, the Aston Martin Valkyrie is a street-legal hypercar. Blurring the line between a track prototype and a road car, it packs a 1,160-horsepower hybrid V12 and generates up to two tons of downforce.

07 / SANT'AGATA BOLOGNESE, ITALY

Lamborghini Revuelto

1,001 hpPower
2.5s0-100 km/h
350+ km/hTop Speed

The Lamborghini Revuelto breaks the mold as the brand's first High Performance Electrified Vehicle. Combining a 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine with three electric motors, it delivers a staggering 1,015 CV (1,001 hp). It rockets from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.5 seconds, with a top speed exceeding 350 km/h.

08 / STUTTGART, GERMANY

Porsche 918 Spyder

887 hpPower
2.6s0-62 mph
918 unitsProduction

The Porsche 918 Spyder pairs a 608-hp, 4.6-liter V8 engine with two electric motors to deliver 887 total horsepower. This plug-in hybrid hypercar rockets from 0 to 62 mph in just 2.6 seconds. Limited to a production run of 918 units, it remains a revolutionary milestone in automotive performance.

09 / INGOLSTADT, GERMANY

Audi R8 V10 Performance

612 hpPower
3.2s0-60 mph
V10 N/AEngine

The Audi R8 V10 Performance marks the end of an era for the iconic, naturally aspirated supercar. It delivers a visceral 602 to 612 horsepower, depending on the specific generation, with blistering acceleration from 0 to 60 mph in roughly 3.2 seconds.

10 / AFFALTERBACH, GERMANY

Mercedes-AMG ONE

1,063 hpPower
275 unitsProduction
F1 Hybrid V6Engine

The Mercedes-AMG ONE is the first street-legal production car equipped with a genuine, race-derived Formula 1 hybrid powertrain. It pairs a highly complex 1.6-liter turbocharged V6 engine with four electric motors to generate a combined 1,063 horsepower. Limited to just 275 units worldwide, the hypercar delivers a true Grand Prix experience.
